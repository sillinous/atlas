## PART V: COMPLIANCE & PERMITS

### Required Permits

| Permit | Issuing Authority | Est. Cost | Timeline |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|----------|
| Business License | City of Newton | $50-100 | 2-4 weeks |
| Food Service License | IL Dept of Public Health | $150-300 | 4-6 weeks |
| Building Permit | Jasper County | $200-500 | 2-4 weeks |
| Sign Permit | City of Newton | $50-100 | 1-2 weeks |
| Fire Inspection | Fire Marshal | Included | Pre-opening |
| Health Inspection | Health Dept | Included | Pre-opening |

### Health Department Requirements (Illinois)

- 3-compartment sink
- Separate hand-washing sink
- Food-safe surfaces
- Proper refrigeration (40°F or below)
- Hot water heater (110°F for handwashing, 180°F for sanitizing)
- Pest control contract
- Employee health policies
- HACCP plan (recommended)

### Accessibility (ADA)

- 36" minimum doorways
- Accessible path to counter
- Accessible restroom
- Counter section at 34" height
- Accessible parking (if applicable)
